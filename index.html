About Us

what we are

Flomm Consult is a legitimately registered travel and hospitality management company licensed by the Registrar Generals Department of Ghana. We started operations in 2012. We have an enduring staff strength equipped with diligence and commitment who are well nurtured to always be at your service. We are your partners in air travel and tourism, ticketing and reservation, visa advisory, your services, hotel reservation and immigration advisory FLOMM SERVICES AND PRODUCTS.
FC is a private organization that provides travel and tourism related service to the public , consultations on determining destination best for client tour, organizing travel data ,preparing travel documentations, providing financial considerations, booking of flight and hotel reservations , planning , describing and arranging itinerary tour packages and promotional travel incentives. Flomm possess a broad knowledge of the world especially popular travel destinations. We provide leisure vacation consultation that best fit your travel needs and wants, we offer knowledge about meetings and conferences as well as seminars with their prespetive locations.
